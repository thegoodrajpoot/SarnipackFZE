Agricultural Nonwoven Products

Our agricultural nonwoven fabrics provide innovative and sustainable solutions to support crop protection, yield improvement, and environmental stewardship.

Product Applications

  • Crop Covers: Maintain optimal soil moisture and temperature, extend growing season, protect from frost and hail.
  • Weed Control Fabrics: Suppress unwanted vegetation while allowing air, water, and nutrients to reach the soil.
  • Shade Nets: Protect plants from excess sunlight and heat stress to improve crop quality.
  • Insect and Pest Barriers: Physically protect crops from damaging insects and birds without pesticides.
  • Root and Fruit Bags: Enhance fruit ripening and root protection with customized nonwoven bags.

Key Benefits

  • Lightweight and Durable: Resists tearing and weather exposure for multiple growing seasons.
  • UV Treated: Protects fabrics and crops from harmful ultraviolet rays.
  • Breathable and Permeable: Allows air and water flow for healthy plant growth.
  • Eco-Friendly Options: Biodegradable and recyclable fabrics reduce environmental impact.
  • Customized Sizes and Colors: Tailored to your specific crop and field requirements.

Why Choose Sarni Pack FZE?

With years of expertise in nonwoven fabric manufacturing, Sarni Pack FZE offers premium quality products designed especially for agricultural applications. Our sustainable materials, cutting-edge technology, and customer-centric approach help farmers increase productivity and promote environmentally responsible farming practices.
